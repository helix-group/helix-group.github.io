Public health partner #2

Contact Case Worker

The long weekend was extremely busy for our health office, the team processed over 1000 positive cases and received over 400 faxes. With these volumes the faxes quickly become unmanageable and unread information continues to build, so FastFax was very helpful to pluck out high priority cases from the ever growing fax file. The FastFax notification made it possible to push the remaining high priority cases out for investigation the same day, as opposed to the file sitting unread until mid-day when we did a sweep of the entire fax folder (roughly 20 hours later).

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to some common questions

  • Can I deploy Fast Fax if I have a different template form?

    Unfortunately, the models that power Fast Fax require adjustment before a new template can be adopted. If you're a state level official looking to setup a state-wide form, please reach out to us and we'll do what we can to help.

  • Does the system require a powerful computer?

    No, Fast Fax is currently deployed on a computer with 8GB of RAM and an Intel® Xeon® Processor E5-2690 v3.

  • Is it freely available?

    Yes, the current system is freely available for use under the MIT open source license.
